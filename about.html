About Us

WHO WE ARE?

Safe, Reliable And Greate Geotechnology Services That Exceeds Your Expectations

Established in 2017 - Phaneretic Services (Phanserv) is a leading geotechnical solutions and services provider in South Africa, renowned for its team of qualified engineering geologists and professional civil technicians.

With eight years of industry expertise, Phanserv has successfully completed 67 projects and earned the satisfaction of 30 clients. Our commitment to excellence and personalized approach enables us to exceed industry standards, offering a comprehensive range of geotechnical services, including foundation investigations, centerline geotechnical investigations, rural, township, and urban development, percolation tests, foundation analysis, construction bridges, borrow pit investigations, and many more.

We are dedicated to ensuring the success and safety of our client's projects through innovative geotechnical solutions. Phanserv establishes itself as the first choice for clients seeking exceptional geotechnical services in South Africa. Trust Phanserv to navigate the complexities of geotechnical projects and provide innovative solutions that ensure the success and safety of your construction endeavors.

Social Responsibility

The company is committed to an active affirmation action policy. Priority is given to historically disadvantaged individuals (HDIs) when recruiting new staff, or to B-BBEE-compliant companies when outsourcing specialist services. Currently, the firm has a 100% HDI staff complement, most of whom are youth. The firm places high emphasis, however, on the compe- tence, experience, and/or development potential of these individuals and firms, so as not to compromise on the quality of the service delivered to the Client.

We are sponsoring a soccer team, donating uniform to a rural school, working on partnering with 2 NPO/NGO's, and did a pro bono job for a community looking to get sponsors for building a Combi-Court (sporting facility).
