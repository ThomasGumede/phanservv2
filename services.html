Our Services

Geotechnical Services we provide

Phaneretic Services (Pty) is a leading provider of comprehensive geotechnical solutions, offering a wide range of specialized services tailored to meet the diverse needs of its clients. With a team of qualified engineering geologists and professional civil technicians, Phanserv ensures the success and safety of various projects with the following geotechnical services:

  • Geotechnical Investigations

    • Phanserv conducts thorough investigations to provide expert recommendations for structures, foundations, roads, and civil construction projects. They utilize advanced techniques to assess soil grading, Atterberg's limits, and compaction testing, ensuring the right foundation design and construction methods.

  • Concrete Strength Testing

    • Ensuring the quality and strength of concrete is crucial in construction projects. Phanserv offers concrete strength testing services to guarantee the structural integrity and durability of buildings and infrastructure.

  • Trial Pit Profiling and Excavations:

    • Phanserv's trial pit profiling and excavation services provide valuable insights into the ground conditions, enabling informed decision-making during the construction process.

  • Borehole and Exploration Drilling:

    • With expertise in borehole and exploration drilling, Phanserv gathers essential data on subsurface conditions, aiding in foundation design and groundwater management.

  • Environmental Impact Assessments and Audits

    • Phanserv conducts thorough environmental impact assessments and audits to ensure compliance with regulations and sustainable practices in development projects.

  • Rural, Township, and Urban Development:

    • Phanserv provides geotechnical services for a diverse range of development projects, including rural, township, and urban areas, meeting the unique challenges each setting presents.

  • Foundation Analysis:

    • Phanserv's foundation analysis services guarantee the selection of the most suitable foundation types and designs for stability and longevity.

  • DCP, DPL, and DPSH Testing:

    • Phanserv employs dynamic cone penetration (DCP), dynamic plate load (DPL), and dynamic probing super heavy (DPSH) testing methods to assess soil properties and compaction in construction sites.

  • Borehole Core Profiling or Percussion Chip Logging:

    • Phanserv's borehole core profiling and percussion chip logging services provide valuable information about the subsurface layers, aiding in geological assessments and foundation design.

  • Percolation Tests:

    • Phanserv conducts percolation tests to assess the rate at which water can infiltrate the soil, vital for the design of drainage systems and wastewater management.
